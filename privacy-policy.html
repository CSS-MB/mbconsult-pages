Privacy Policy

For: NetSuite ↔ Microsoft 365 Integration Hub

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the NetSuite ↔ Microsoft 365 Integration Hub (the "App") published by MB Consult, LLC ("we," "us," "our"). It does not describe general website practices for mbconsult.io; it specifically covers the App and any related services used to operate the App.

Data We Access and Use

The App integrates your NetSuite tenant with Microsoft 365 services (e.g., Microsoft Graph, Teams, Outlook, SharePoint, and Power BI). The App may access business records such as customers, vendors, transactions, files, and metadata necessary to fulfill the integration functions you configure.

We do not collect, store, or sell personal information outside of your own NetSuite and Microsoft 365 tenants. Processing occurs within your controlled environments and any Azure resources you provision for the App (e.g., Azure Functions/Storage if used).

Data Processing Location

Data is processed within your NetSuite and Microsoft 365 tenants and any Azure region(s) you select. We do not transfer your data to MB Consult, LLC systems for persistent storage.

Sub-processors

The App relies on platform services you control (NetSuite, Microsoft 365, optional Azure components). MB Consult, LLC does not engage additional sub-processors for your data.

Security

Access to NetSuite and Microsoft APIs uses platform-standard authentication (e.g., OAuth 2.0 via Entra ID and NetSuite). You control roles/permissions. We recommend least-privilege roles and IP/session restrictions where available.

Retention

The App does not retain personal data outside your environments. Any logs or artifacts generated in your Azure resources follow your retention policies.

Your Rights & Requests

Because the App runs in your own tenants, requests to access, correct, or delete data should be executed by your tenant administrators using native platform capabilities. If you require assistance operating the App to fulfill a request, contact us at support@mbconsult.io.

Children's Data

The App is for business use and is not directed to children.

Changes

We may update this policy to reflect operational, legal, or regulatory changes. Updates will be posted at https://mbconsult.io/privacy-policy with a new effective date.
